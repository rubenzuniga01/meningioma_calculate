Recurrence-Free Survival (RFS) Calculator for Meningiomas ℹ️ Only applicable for surgical cases

Disclaimer:
The following calculator has not been externally validated, uses published data from several studies (systematic review and meta-analysis yet to be published in World Neurosurgery) and therefore should not be used to provide medical advice. This tool should only be used for research or educational purposes, encouraging the performance of large multicenter prospective studies, as occurs with IMPACT or CRASH in TBI.

Authors:
